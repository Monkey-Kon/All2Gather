About Us

We are All2Gather, the site where you can get out of your inactivity and start moving again. We make sports an easy expe rience for you so you can have all the fun you have without the difficulty of planning. Playing sports has never been this easy!

Our Vision

Athletes teaming up through their shared love for sports, them pushing forward towards their goals and having fun along the way

Our Mission

We want to support casual athletes in their desire to connect through exercising by moving their focus from planning solely to enjoyment.
